send me on my way

Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) is located at 1 Lomb Memorial Drive, Rochester, NY 14623. Approximately 20 minutes from Greater Rochester International Airport and the Amtrak and Bus stations, there are multiple ways to travel to Rochester and TAW. For information on transportation, use these links:

VISITROCHESTER.COM →

RIT.EDU →

Once you arrive on campus, signs will direct you to parking in lots E and H and to the check-in at Booth Hall (Building 7A). If you arrive on Friday, you'll need to get a visitor pass from the Welcome Center located on Lomb Memorial Drive, just past the roundabout.

welcome to TAW 2017

Check-in

Check-in begins at 9am on Friday morning. The opening reception begins at 11:45am. At check-in, you'll get a lanyard and name tag, which you'll need for entry to any Thought at Work event.

You'll also pick up a program brochure for a detailed view of the conference and schedule.

the essentials

We've assembled a checklist for your planning needs -

Clothing
Check the weather forecast in advance. Rochester's weather can be unpredictable. The conference is two days, so bring at least two pairs of underwear.
Materials
We recommend that you bring a sketchbook with a writing utensil, along with your smartphone.
Cameras
Cameras are welcome at most events.
Your work
There are scheduled portfolio reviews and networking events. We suggest that you bring work samples and business cards.
